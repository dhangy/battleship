Basic Description of the Game!

This is an online 1 player version of the classic Battleship Game!

To get started you can either select the button that says "Place Ships Randomly?" or you can click on the buttons down below to setup your own ships!

if you want to setup your own ships you need to do the following:

  1. click on a cell to select where your ship will start! (the ship will be filled in from top to bottom or left to right of your point!)
  2. then select a ship to be put on the grid from the named buttons below
  3. then select an orientation button either vertical or horizontal
  4. lastly you must click the place ship button and your ship will be placed for you!
  5. Repeat until you have placed all 5 ships and the game will start for you!
